# Manage accounts in LarePass

Use this guide to import an existing Olares ID, switch between accounts, or remove accounts from the current device.

## Import an account

Import an existing Olares ID with its 12-word mnemonic phrase when you use LarePass on a new device or another client.

:::info Have your mnemonic phrase ready
To import an Olares ID, you need its 12-word mnemonic phrase. If you do not have it, [back up the mnemonic phrase](back-up-mnemonics.md) on a device where the account is already available.
:::

### On mobile

1. Open the LarePass app.
2. Tap your profile avatar.
3. On the **Switch account** page, tap **Add account** at the bottom.
4. Tap **Import account**.
5. Enter the 12-word mnemonic phrase for your Olares ID.

   ![Import account on mobile device](/images/manual/larepass/import-account-mobile.png#bordered)

### On desktop

1. Open the LarePass desktop client.
2. Click your profile avatar.
3. Click **Switch account**.
4. Click **Add account** at the bottom.
5. Enter the 12-word mnemonic phrase for your Olares ID.

   ![Import account on desktop](/images/manual/larepass/import-account-desktop.png#bordered)

## Switch accounts

If you have added multiple Olares IDs in LarePass, you can switch between them at any time.

### On mobile

1. Open the **Switch account** page in one of the following ways:

   - **From the main interface**: Tap your profile avatar in the top-left corner.

      ![Open Switch account from the main interface](/images/manual/larepass/switch-account-mobile1.png#bordered)

   - **During account creation**: Tap the account icon in the top-right corner.

      ![Open Switch account during account creation](/images/manual/larepass/switch-account-mobile3.png#bordered)

2. Tap the account you want to use.

   ![Select an account](/images/manual/larepass/switch-account-mobile4.png#bordered)

### On desktop

1. Click your profile avatar in the top-left corner, then click **Switch account**.
2. Click the account you want to use.

   ![Switch accounts on desktop](/images/manual/larepass/switch-account-desktop1.png#bordered)

## Delete accounts

Remove an account from LarePass when you no longer want to keep it on the current device.

:::warning Back up your mnemonic phrase first
You can delete an account only after you have [backed up its mnemonic phrase](back-up-mnemonics.md). Without the backup, LarePass prevents you from deleting the account.
:::

### On mobile

1. Open the LarePass app.
2. Tap your profile avatar.
3. Open the **Switch account** page.
4. Tap <i class="material-symbols-outlined">checklist</i> in the top-right corner.
5. Select the accounts you want to delete.
6. Tap <i class="material-symbols-outlined">delete</i> in the top-right corner, then confirm the deletion.

   ![Delete accounts on mobile device](/images/manual/larepass/delete-account-mobile.png#bordered)

### On desktop

1. Click your profile avatar in the top-left corner, then click **Settings**.
2. Scroll down to **Account**, then click **Manage account**.
3. Click **Delete account** to delete the current account, then confirm the deletion.

   ![Delete accounts on desktop](/images/manual/larepass/delete-account-desktop.png#bordered)
