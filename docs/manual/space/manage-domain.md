## Remove a user

You can only remove a user if they have not yet created an Olares ID under your domain.

1. In Olares Space, go to **Domain management**, and click **View** next to your domain.
2. Find the username you want to remove, and click **Delete**.
3. Click **Confirm** in the pop-up dialog.

## What's next

- Once users receive their Olares ID and password, they can [join the organization in LarePass](../larepass/create-org-account.md#join-an-existing-organization).
- If a member will join the admin's existing Olares instance, see [Manage your team](../olares/settings/manage-team.md) for details.
